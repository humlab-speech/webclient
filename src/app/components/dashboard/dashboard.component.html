About

Visible Speech is a collection of research tools for speech analysis. If you do not have an account, you may visit the demo-site for a tour of its capabilties. If you wish to setup a project on the live system, please get in touch with us at support@humlab.umu.se.

Visible Speech is being developed by Umeå university.
